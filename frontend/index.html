    About SarkarSathi ADK

    Bridging the gap between citizens and government welfare schemes using AI

    The Problem

    India has 1000+ welfare schemes — yet most eligible citizens never discover or apply for them. Barriers include language, literacy, complex eligibility, and lack of awareness.

    • ~70% of eligible citizens don't claim benefits they're entitled to
    • Schemes are spread across dozens of Central and State portals
    • Eligibility criteria are complex and multi-dimensional
    • Limited regional language and rural support
    Our Solution

    SarkarSathi uses Google's ADK to deploy 7 specialized AI agents that evaluate eligibility, rank schemes by benefit value, and provide transparent recommendations in your language.

    • AI matching across age, income, state, gender, education, occupation and category
    • Ranked recommendations with benefit score and "why recommended" explanations
    • Near-eligibility guidance — shows exactly what's missing
    • 11 Indian languages with voice input support

    How Recommendation Works

    1
    Profile Analysis

    The Profile Agent extracts structured data from free-text or form input using Gemini 2.5 Flash.

    2
    Eligibility Checking

    The Eligibility Agent runs deterministic rules — no hallucinations, guaranteed accuracy.

    3
    Benefit Ranking

    The Recommendation Agent scores schemes by financial value, subsidies, scholarships and user relevance.

    4
    Multilingual Reply

    The Explainability and Multilingual agents produce a grounded, transparent explanation in the citizen's language.
